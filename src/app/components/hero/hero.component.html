Lack access to therapeutic nutrition

Non‑communicable diseases such as diabetes, chronic kidney disease, cardiovascular disease, and cancer are rising rapidly across Africa. Many patients lack access to clinically designed therapeutic nutrition that supports medical treatment and improves recovery.

Nutriomedics addresses this gap by developing scientifically formulated nutraceutical solutions that complement clinical care.

Why Hospitals Should Choose Nutriomedics Enteral Nutrition

Nutriomedics enteral formulas are designed to support hospitals and healthcare providers in delivering effective, safe, and affordable nutrition therapy for patients who cannot meet their nutritional needs through normal oral intake.

Clinical Advantages

  • ✓ Balanced macronutrient composition for clinical nutrition therapy
  • ✓ Adequate high‑quality protein to support recovery and tissue repair
  • ✓ Optimized energy density for patients with increased metabolic needs
  • ✓ Carefully balanced electrolytes and micronutrients

Operational Advantages for Hospitals

  • ✓ Designed for easy preparation and administration
  • ✓ Suitable for tube feeding and oral nutrition support
  • ✓ Consistent formulation for reliable clinical use
  • ✓ Can be integrated into hospital nutrition protocols

Economic Advantage

  • ✓ Imported enteral formulas are often expensive and difficult to sustain
  • ✓ Locally developed, cost‑effective clinical nutrition solutions
  • ✓ Reduces dependence on imported products
  • ✓ Maintains high nutritional standards
