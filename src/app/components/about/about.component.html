About Nutriomedics

Clinical Experience Meets Nutritional Innovation

Nutriomedics Company Limited develops clinically‑driven nutraceutical products and medical nutrition therapies designed to improve patient outcomes and complement modern medical treatment. Founded in Dodoma, Tanzania, we integrate clinical medicine, nutrition science, and product innovation to produce therapeutic nutrition solutions that are safe, affordable, and locally adaptable.

Our innovations include Diab Pro for diabetes management, locally formulated enteral pre‑digested nutritional support for critically ill patients, high‑calorie high‑protein products for children living with HIV/AIDS, and neuropathic relief nutrition products. We collaborate with COSTECH, the Tanzania Startup Association, the University of Dodoma, and public health programs such as EGPAF, ICAP, and USAID.
